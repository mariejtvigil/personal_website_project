introduction

This document outlines the User Experience and Interaction Design for the proposed “jtdesignsolutions.com” Personal Website Project. “jtdesignsolutions.com” is a professional portfolio website with a focus on front-end web development. The site is designed for serving professionals and businesses within any industry within the New Mexico community or nationwide. Target audience to include: startup or small businesses, individuals that have a need for online marketing, as well as serving our fine art community.

The primary goal of the site is to highlight my servers to professional industries, connect social media profiles in one location, and to be visible in an online community. The secondary goal of the site is to showcase a portfolio of my current and past work within the print and online community. Contact information as well as a contact form to email me with questions or concerns will be available to users of the site.

persona

Name: Lydia Flores
Age: 39

Profession: Fine Artist that is looking to create an online presence by creating a website that highlights her current and present work.

Technology: Lydia is a fine artist that used her hands to create art. Her current knowledge of computers is for email, taxes, writing letters, user of social media, and online shopping.

Attitudes and Behaviors: Lydia is a calm and patient person that has four children. Her time is dedicated to her family, her church, and her art. She volunteers for the local public schools that her children attend.

Frustrations and Needs: Lydia is a creative unknown fine artist. She wishes to reach a larger audience outside her city and state. She desires to become known nationally within her country as well as the world. She has trouble selling her art in her local community due to it's small size and poor economical situation.

Goals: Lydia's goal is to attract attention from fine art retail businesses and museums in hope that they will display and sell her art. She also wishes to have blog on her website so she can share your insights and knowledge with others.

user case

  • User searches for graphic artist within google.
  • Google lists a variety of graphic artist that have keywords for web design and web development.
  • Marie's website is listing close to the top of the Google search on the first page.
  • User clicks on Marie's website link on Google page.
  • Marie's website appears in the browser window displaying her websites home page.
  • The user is welcomed with a small bio about Marie and her work.
  • The user can than chose to visit other pages within her website such as the contact page, or learn more about Marie on the about us page, or review additional work within the portfolio page.
  • User likes Marie's work and wishes to contact her.
  • User clicks the contact button within navigation bar.
  • Contact information and an optional email form is available on the page within the browser's viewport.
  • User wishes to write Marie an email with a short message and their contact information.
  • User closes the browser.

proposed site map

This Minimum Viable Website will consist of 4 top-level pages total. No sub-pages or sub-navigation.

  • Home Page
  • About Us
  • Portfolio
  • Contact
  • Possibly add a page for “Serves” that illustrate available serves to meet client’s business needs.

Home Page: The user is welcomed with a small bio about Marie and her work. Graphic image or images of her work will be represented here.

About Us: The user reviews additional information about Marie and her design studio. The user can watch a video about Marie or related topic that is linked to YouTube. There is also a calendar on this page that demonstrates Marie’s art shows and related events. Hyperlinks to press releases and other storys that highlight Marie’s talents.

Portfolio: This page demonstrates that Marie is an outstanding artist in her field. Images of her artwork will display work completed for previous clients and possible completed fine art as well.

Contact Contact information and an optional email form is available for the user to contract Marie.
